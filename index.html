Background

I am a certified Geographic Information Systems (GIS) Professional (GISP) with over five years of experience in the GIS field including work in environmental consulting, defense contracting, natural resources, and public safety. I am currently working for the Commonwealth of Virginia as a GIS Analyst/IT Specialist and manage and administer an enterprise GIS to include: web servers, GIS servers, enterprise geodatabases, batch processing, and web mapping applications. Additionally, I am a master’s degree candidate (2019) in the field of Information Management Systems and have about three years of experience in Python geospatial development and full stack web development using several modern software and technology stacks.
