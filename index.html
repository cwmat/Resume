What I Do ?

I am an Analyst/GIS Technician II at Marstel-Day, LLC which is an environmental and conservation consulting firm and have four years of GIS experience. My main responsibilities at Marstel-Day include conducting GIS analysis using ESRI and open source GIS technologies, Python programming, developing web GIS products, research/technical writing about environmental and land use topics, and developing new ways to manage and analyze GIS data to better support our clients.

My background is in environmental science/GIS and I received my B.S. in Environmental Studies from Virginia Commonwealth University and a certificate in GIS from George Mason University. In addition to my environmental background, I am skilled in the realm of computer science/programming and am currently working part-time on an M.A. in Software Engineering at Harvard Extension School (online).
